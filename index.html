Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors game! Here are the rules:
  1. Rock beats Scissors.
  2. Paper beats Rock.
  3. Scissors beats Paper.
  4. If both you and the computer choose the same, it's a tie!
How to play:
  1. Click one of the buttons to choose your move.
  2. The computer will randomly choose its move.
  3. See the result: You Win, Computer Wins, or It's a tie!
